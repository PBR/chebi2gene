The goal of this tool is to provide an easy way to link a specified molecule to the Tomato genome.

For any given CHEBI identifier, it will look up the Rhea database. It will search all the reactions in which this molecule is involved. Find all the protein involved in these reactions as well and retrieve the pathway of these protein as well as the gene from the Tomato genome linked to them.
